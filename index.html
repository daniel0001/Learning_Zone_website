About the learning zone

I am John Glynn ARPS and have been teaching photography and computing classes for the Edinburgh Adult Education programme for over ten years. I have a BA First Class Honours degree in Photography and Film from Edinburgh Napier University and a Post-graduate Teaching Qualification in Further Education (PQFE) form Stirling University. I am also an Associate member of the Royal Photographic Society.

Introduction

Welcome to the Learning Zone. On this site you will find information regarding my forthcoming classes on Photography and Computing. There are also lesson handouts/information that can be downloaded as PDF or .doc files and printed out. This information is only available to students who have attended my classes.
